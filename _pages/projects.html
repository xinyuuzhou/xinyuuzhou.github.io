Assessment of public response on Weibo (China's Twitter) during COVID-19 pandemic in China.
Supervisors: Prof. Zhiyuan Hou (Fudan University) and Prof. Leesa Lin (LSHTM)
- Collected over 10,000,000 Weibo posts regarding COVID-19 outbreak. - Assessed public response, including sentiment, risk perception, anxiety, using Machine Learning and Natural Language Processing Tools. - As co-first author or second author, two manuscripts of mine are currently submitted or under review.
