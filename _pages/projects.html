SRS_and_Stratified_sampling
by Xinyu Zhou
Homework, Stats 262 Theory and Practice of Sample Survey (Doctoral Elective Course), Department of Statistics, ICS@UC Irvine. This project used dataset "Student performance in California schools". In this project, I sampled 60 schools for a pilot study to estimate stratum variances, and use Neyman’s allocation to sample 600 schools to estimate the population mean. Statistical package such as R is applied in this assignment.
